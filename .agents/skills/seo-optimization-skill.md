# SEO Optimization Skill

Use for technical SEO, on-page SEO, local SEO, schema, sitemap, robots, internal linking and AI search visibility tasks.

Workflow:

1. Inspect page, metadata, schema and crawl rules.
2. Identify indexation, canonical, performance and content gaps.
3. Prioritize fixes by ranking and conversion impact.
4. Produce implementation notes and QA checks.
